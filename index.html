Who We Are

Simplifying delivery so scientists can focus on the science.

Non-viral delivery is one of the most persistent bottlenecks in cell and gene therapy development. Existing tools are complex, poorly documented, and built for specialists — not the broad community of researchers and developers driving the field forward.

BioPharma was founded to change that. We build modular delivery systems that are easy to integrate, reproducible across labs, and designed to work with real-world workflows — whether you're at a university bench or inside a CDMO's GMP facility.

We work closely with the people using our tools. The site is a starting point — we'd rather have the conversation in person.

The Platform

How our platform works.

01 — The Problem

Viral vectors are not enough

Viral delivery dominates the field — but it comes with manufacturing complexity, immunogenicity risk, and payload constraints that limit what's possible. Non-viral alternatives exist, but most require deep expertise to use reliably.

02 — Our Approach

Modular, non-viral delivery

Our platform provides configurable, non-viral delivery components that can be adapted to a range of cargo types — nucleic acids, proteins, and beyond — without requiring custom engineering for each application.

03 — Who It's For

Built for developers, not just experts

Designed for research labs, biotech developers, and CDMO process teams. Clear protocols, predictable performance, and modular components that fit into existing workflows without a steep learning curve.
